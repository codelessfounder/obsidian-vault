### 1. Entity & Licence Stack

- **Parent/OpCo** → **Costa Rica** (0% corp. tax if offshore). Holds **Anjouan licence**.
- **Licence** → **Anjouan** (fast, ~6 weeks; whitelisted by Evolution; light post-licence compliance).
- **Payment-Agent Co.** → **Cyprus** (contracts PSPs/banks; handles fiat/crypto settlement; taxed only on small service fee).

**Cashflow:** Player payments → PSPs → Cyprus bank → remitted to Costa Rica parent.

---

### 2. Timeline (End-to-End)

**Month 0–1**

- Form **Costa Rica parent** (5 days + docs prep).
- Kick off **RNG certification** (~2–8 weeks, €5k).
- Register **Cyprus payment-agent** (~12 days).

**Month 1–2**

- **Anjouan licence application** submitted (approval ~6 weeks).

**Month 2–3**

- **Licence issued**.
- **Bank account onboarding (Cyprus)** (+30 days).
- **PSP contracting** (Visa, Mastercard, Apple/Google Pay, PayID AU).
- **Crypto rails** integrated.

**Go-live in ~3 months (90 days).**

### 3. Markets & Go-to-Market

- **Initial markets**: Canada, Nordics, New Zealand, South Africa.
- **Later expansion**: Chile, Peru, Eastern Europe, CIS.
- **Avoid initially**: US, UK, AU, DE, FR, NL, Spain, India, Brazil.

### 4. Cost Summary

- **Costa Rica + Anjouan licence**: €32.9k
- **Cyprus entity setup**: €5.5k
- **Bank onboarding support**: €3k
- **RNG certification**: €5k per game
**Total initial compliance outlay ≈ €41k**

### 5. Key Compliance Requirements

- **RNG Certification**: Mandatory for Disco’s in-house games.
- **Banking/PSPs**: Must route via Cyprus (offshore entities not accepted).
- **Taxation**: Cyprus taxed on small service fee (12.5%); Costa Rica remains zero-tax.
- **Geo-fencing**: Restricted markets blocked at launch.
