A vested interest market is a part who would gain disproportionately if your company succeeds in an economic, strategic or cultural way. 

Disco’s VIM (Vested Interest Market) is anyone who either:

1. makes money from online gambling already 
2. benefits from a cultural shift towards gamified consumer behaviour 

Disco’s VIM are: 

## **1. High-volume affiliates in online gambling**

These people *print money* when a new casino succeeds.

### Why they’re vested:

- They make recurring revenue on every player you acquire.
- They are *always* searching for new, high-converting casinos to promote.
- A casino with a contrarian brand + narrative? That spikes conversion.
- They have cash. They invest quietly.

### Who they actually are:

- Large “casino comparison” site owners

- Influencer networks that push casino traffic

- Streamers with large gambling audiences

These are **your best early investors** because they directly profit from Disco’s success.

These people are frequently angel investors.
