## Investor Q&A:

## **Market & Vision**

**Q: Isn’t the online casino market already saturated?**

**A:** Yes — but it’s saturated with *copy-paste* casino experiences. Disco isn’t “just another slots site”; it reframes gaming around *real-world purchases*. Instead of abstract chips, users wager for the chance to get their coffee, sneakers, or dinner free. That contextualisation unlocks a new audience (Gen Z / Millennials) who are less drawn to legacy casino sites but are highly responsive to gamified commerce.

**Q: Why would people use Disco instead of Stake, DraftKings, etc.?**

**A:** Stake/DraftKings monetise purely on cash gambling. Disco makes play *personal and fun* by linking outcomes to things users are already buying. It isn’t a replacement — it’s a new category of *commerce-driven entertainment*.

**Q: What’s your TAM / SAM / SOM?**

**A:** TAM is the ~$100B+ global online gambling market. Our SAM is the $28B segment of online players aged 18–35 engaging in gamified or mobile-first platforms. Our SOM in 5 years is 0.5–2% of SAM ($140M–$680M annual revenues), achievable given average ARPU of ~$400 and target acquisition of 350k–1.7M users.

---

## **Product & Experience**

**Q: Isn’t this just gambling with a new skin?**

**A:** Framing is everything. Users aren’t chasing abstract winnings — they’re contextualising play around things they already wanted to buy. That creates *less predatory intent* and more *playfulness*. A flip can mean a free hoodie or dinner, not just money in or out.

**Q: How do you keep people coming back?**

**A:** Disco builds sticky loops: (1) Outcomes are inherently shareable (“my coffee was free”), (2) Progression systems (XP, loyalty multipliers) add depth, (3) AI-driven contextualisation ensures outcomes feel personal, not random.

**Q: What’s your moat?**

**A:** Two layers:

1. **AI moat**: Proprietary vision pipeline that interprets receipts, carts, or product photos to contextualise wagers. Competitors can’t just plug in a Vision API — we’ve designed multimodal reasoning + structured JSON to slot into our wagering system.
2. **UGC moat**: Gameplay outcomes are viral by design. $150 per UGC video + AI content loops → scalable distribution.

---

## **Monetisation & Economics**

**Q: How does Disco make money?**

**A:** Each play has a fixed house edge of ~10–12% (similar to casino RTPs). Unlike payments or SaaS, this creates *predictable gross gaming revenue per wager*.

**Q: What’s the CAC / payback profile?**

**A:** Our CAC is ~$120 (in line with online casino averages). With ARPU at ~$400, payback is within the first 3–4 months. Viral loops (UGC + creator content) reduce CAC over time.

**Q: Isn’t the model too risky if players “win” too often?**

**A:** We manage payout volatility with dynamic RTP and game modes (e.g. safe vs high risk). Guardrails ensure house margin is protected at the cohort level, even if some users hit short-term lucky streaks.

---

## **Legal & Regulatory**

**Q: Isn’t this gambling? Will you get shut down?**

**A:** Disco is framed as *entertainment gaming*, not as a payment processor. Crypto is non-cashable, non-withdrawable, and used only for play. We sit in a legal grey zone closer to claw machines and loot boxes. Additionally, we’ll employ jurisdictional geo-fencing and regulatory arbitrage (e.g., licensing in Anjouan, Curacao, or Philippines) to stay compliant.

**Q: What about consumer protection / problem gambling?**

**A:** We’re proactive: session limits, cooling-off periods, provably fair outcomes, and transparent RTP disclosures. This isn’t a dark casino — it’s an entertainment layer.

---

## **Traction & Growth**

**Q: Do you have proof people actually want this?**

**A:** Early demo testing shows strong engagement uplift: +12% checkout engagement vs control, with users explicitly reporting the “thrill” of play as the hook. Waitlist is growing with organic interest, validating demand.

**Q: How will you scale distribution?**

**A:** Growth stack:

- **UGC content** — outcomes are inherently shareable.
- **Influencers** — B-list musicians, streamers, and creators.
- **Community channels** — Discord/TikTok loops.
This is *content-first growth* rather than ads-only spend.

---

## **Team & Execution**

**Q: Why you?**

**A:** Founder-led, with both **technical execution** (full-stack build in React/TypeScript/Supabase, integrated AI pipelines) and **product intuition** (identifying the gap between e-commerce and gaming). Lean, execution-heavy, and built to ship fast.

**Q: What keeps you up at night?**

**A:** Two things: (1) getting regulatory framing exactly right, and (2) ensuring we maintain entertainment-first optics, not gambling-first. Both are actively mitigated with legal structuring and careful UX design.
