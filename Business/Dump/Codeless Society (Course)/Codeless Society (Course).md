*To create a Bubble development academy for new employees with no prior experience, the lessons should gradually introduce them to the platform, focusing on both conceptual understanding and practical skills. By the end of the 5-hour introductory program, you can assess their potential to become strong Bubble developers based on how well they grasp the platform's core concepts and basic workflows.*

### Overall Evaluation Criteria:

4. **Technical Understanding**: How well do they grasp the core concepts?
5. **Problem-Solving Skills**: How do they approach tasks and troubleshoot issues?
6. **Attention to Detail**: Are their workflows efficient and error-free?
7. **Creativity**: How do they enhance basic projects? Are they finding ways to go beyond the basic instructions?
8. **Responsiveness to Feedback**: How well do they learn from corrections or guidance during the hands-on sessions?

These lessons provide a good foundation in Bubble and will help identify which employees have the aptitude to become strong developers. You can then move forward with advanced topics based on their performance.
