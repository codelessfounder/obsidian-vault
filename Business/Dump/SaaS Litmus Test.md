Comprehensive SaaS Product Evaluation Checklist
This document outlines an extensive litmus test for assessing the quality and effectiveness of a
SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) product. The checklist covers multiple dimensions, including product
value, usability, security, and business scalability.

1. Core Product Value & Fit
- Does it solve a real problem that users are willing to pay for?
- Does it offer unique value compared to competitors?
- Is the product aligned with the needs of its target audience?
- Is the customer churn rate low (below 5%)?
2. User Experience (UX) & Interface
- Is it easy to use with minimal onboarding?
- Are core features accessible within a few clicks?
- Does it provide a seamless mobile experience?
- Does it adhere to accessibility standards (e.g., WCAG)?
3. Performance and Reliability
- Does the product provide at least 99.9% uptime?
- Are load times fast, even under heavy usage?
- Is data syncing smooth across devices and users?
4. Security & Compliance
- Does it use end-to-end encryption?
- Is it compliant with regulations like GDPR or HIPAA?
- Does it offer role-based access control (RBAC) and multi-factor authentication?
5. Integrations and Ecosystem
- Does it integrate with essential tools (e.g., CRM, ERP)?
- Is there an API for custom workflows?
- Does it have a marketplace for plugins or add-ons?
6. Pricing Model and Scalability
- Is the pricing model transparent with no hidden fees?
- Can the product scale with user growth?
- Are there flexible pricing tiers?
7. Customer Support and Documentation
- Is there 24/7 support available?
- Does it offer a comprehensive knowledge base?
- Is there a community for peer support and feedback?
8. Analytics and Reporting
- Does it provide insights into user activity?
- Are there customizable reports for tracking KPIs?
9. Business Sustainability and Reputation
- What do reviews on platforms like G2 or Trustpilot say?
- Is the SaaS provider financially stable?
- Is there a risk of the product being discontinued?
10. Feature Velocity and Innovation
- Does the product receive regular updates?
- Is the roadmap publicly available?
