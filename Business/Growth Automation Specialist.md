About Us

Founded in Sydney, Australia, Software Combined offers a forever home for successful B2B tech businesses. We invest in companies with a strong culture and clear vision — not just protecting what makes them special, but amplifying it to help them grow in the right direction.

With a growing portfolio of 13 companies across A/NZ and Europe, we are a lean, high-trust team that moves quickly and values people who take initiative and get things done.

The Opportunity

This is a hands-on, technically creative role for someone who gets genuinely excited about automation, AI, and making things work better. You’ll own a suite of key business systems and have real freedom to experiment, build, and innovate.

Your work will touch every corner of Software Combined’s business, from how we find and evaluate acquisition targets, to how our portfolio companies grow. You’ll be encouraged to bring ideas, test them, and put them into practice. If you want a role where you can shape the way a business operates and build something genuinely new, this is it.

What You’ll Do

**Systems Administration**

- Own and administer the HQ technology stack, including Monday.com, Attio (CRM), Instantly, HeyReach, Clay, Apollo, Make.com, N8N, Microsoft 365, and AI tools.
- Keep systems running reliably, maintain data hygiene, and support the team with day-to-day tooling needs.
- Manage HQ software subscriptions and collaborate with our finance team and vCISO to manage costs and security.

**Automation & Innovation**

- Build and evolve automation workflows across our core functions: Prospecting, M&A, Portfolio support, and Finance.
- Experiment freely with new tools, AI models, and approaches to find better ways of doing things.
- Translate manual, repetitive processes into scalable systems; find automation opportunities the team hasn’t spotted yet.
- Compile and maintain ExCo dashboards, automating reporting pipelines wherever possible.

**Prospecting & M&A**

- Administer and optimise outbound prospecting campaigns via Instantly, HeyReach, and Clay.
- Support city blitz campaigns and deal activity with data operations and workflow automation.

**Portfolio Company Collaboration**

- Work directly with portfolio companies to apply AI, automation, and technical expertise to their growth challenges.
- Learn from what our portfolio businesses are doing, bring those learnings back to HQ, and contribute to a shared ecosystem of innovation.
- Support GTM workflows for portfolio companies, including CRM automation and go-to-market tooling.

Our Expectations

- **Technically hands-on: **You build things yourself. You’re comfortable in workflow automation tools, setting up a custom MCP, can troubleshoot an API call, and are curious about AI and what it can do.
- **Self-directed: **You don’t wait to be told what to automate. You spot inefficiencies and go after them.
- **Experimental mindset: **You try things, learn from them, and iterate. You see failure as part of the process.
- **Excellent communicator: **You can explain what you’ve built and why it matters to non-technical stakeholders.
- **Collaborative: **You’re energised by working with a small, high-calibre team and by bringing value to the businesses in our portfolio.

What We Offer You

- Real autonomy and the trust to experiment, your ideas will be heard and backed.
- A front-row seat to M&A, portfolio operations, and growth strategy across a diverse set of software businesses.
- Hands-on work with cutting-edge AI and automation tools, with the freedom to push the boundaries of what’s possible.
- A close-knit, high-trust team where your work is visible and your impact is real.
- Direct collaboration with portfolio companies, giving you breadth of exposure across multiple businesses and markets.

Who You Are

- 2–5 years of experience in a growth operations, business automations, or GTM engineer role.
- Hands-on experience with automation tools (N8N or similar), CRM (Attio, Hubspot), outbound tools (Clay), and various other workflow and business systems.
- Familiarity with AI tools and a genuine curiosity about applying them to real business problems.
- Comfortable owning systems end-to-end, from initial setup to ongoing optimisation.
- Based in Sydney, with the ability to work from our office regularly.
