Benefits of the inventory link: 

- **Increased Exposure** – Dealers can showcase their entire inventory across multiple platforms (Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook, etc.), reaching a wider audience effortlessly.
- **Seamless Customer Experience** – Customers can browse all available watches in one place without needing to visit multiple listings or ask for individual watch details.
- **Real-Time Updates** – The link reflects the most up-to-date inventory, ensuring customers always see available watches without outdated listings.
- **Faster Sales Cycle** – Instead of manually sending watch details to potential buyers, dealers can simply share one link, streamlining the buying process.
- **Stronger Social Media Presence** – Having an inventory link in an Instagram bio or WhatsApp status allows for **passive marketing**, letting interested buyers browse at their convenience.
- **Lead Generation & Conversion** – By embedding the link in posts and ads, dealers can **drive traffic** to their inventory and convert social media followers into buyers.
- **Reduced Admin Work** – Saves time by eliminating the need for dealers to constantly update buyers on available watches.
- **Better Customer Retention** – Encourages repeat visits, as buyers can check the inventory link periodically for new arrivals.
