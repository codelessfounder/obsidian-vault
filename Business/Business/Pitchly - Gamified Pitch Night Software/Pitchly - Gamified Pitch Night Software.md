Benefits:

1. **Gamification of Pitch Nights**: Giving the audience a fixed “investment” amount introduces a fun, engaging, and competitive mechanic that gets people thinking like real investors.
2. **Better Feedback Loop for Startups**: Founders often leave pitch nights with vague applause and little to work with. This software turns passive observation into structured, actionable feedback and potential leads
3. **Lead Generation for Startups and VCs**: Real investors in the crowd can self-identify, which turns your tool into a **deal flow capture engine** for both founders and funders.
4. **Audience Engagement**: Your tool increases attentiveness and participation. Startup profiles mean the audience can refer back to details of the startups they’ve seen. 


Who is it for?

- Startup pitch nights (like the one you went to)
- Demo days at accelerators/incubators (e.g., Startmate, Antler)
- University innovation showcases
- Corporate internal innovation contests
- Angel networks and early-stage VC events

User journey (event host):

1. Sign in / create account (or use admin access link for MVP simplicity).
2. Create a new event with name, date, venue, and startup list.
3. Generate event access link or QR code and distribute to audience.

User journey (audience member):

1. Joins through QR code or access link.
2. Receives a virtual investment amount (for example, $50,000).
3. Reviews startup profiles before allocating funds.
4. Distributes all virtual funds across one or more startups.
5. Submits optional startup feedback and optional contact details.
6. Finalises votes and receives a confirmation screen.

MVP feature priorities (MoSCoW):

- Must have: frictionless audience join, virtual allocation mechanic, startup browse view, feedback capture, optional contact form, event setup/admin flow, and exportable outcomes.
- Should have: real-time leaderboard, audience recap email, and reminder nudges before exit.
- Could have: startup result portal, public big-screen results mode, and lightweight gamified spend animations.
- Won't have (MVP): real-money investment flows, AI summaries, and deep startup profile management.
