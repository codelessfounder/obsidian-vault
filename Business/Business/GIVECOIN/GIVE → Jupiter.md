To get your **GIVE token** on **Jupiter** (a popular decentralized exchange aggregator on Solana), follow these steps:

---

### **1. Ensure Your Token is Minted and Verified**

- Your token (`GIVE`) must be successfully minted on the Solana blockchain.
- Verify that your token address (e.g., `79FqiUge64bxkh47zZoft6Fumjx7mEwEa1s1bgybtKXK`) is correct.

---

### **2. Add Liquidity to a Decentralized Exchange (DEX)**

Jupiter aggregates liquidity from Solana DEXs like **Raydium** and **Orca**. For Jupiter to recognize and trade your token, you must add liquidity to one of these platforms:

### **A. Provide Liquidity on Raydium**

1. Go to [Raydium.io](https://raydium.io/).
2. Connect your wallet (e.g., Phantom) that holds the GIVE token and SOL.
3. Navigate to the **"Liquidity"** tab.
4. Select **"Add Liquidity"**.
    - Pair your token (`GIVE`) with SOL or a stablecoin like USDC.
    - Enter the amount of GIVE and the paired token you want to provide as liquidity.
5. Confirm the transaction.

### **B. Provide Liquidity on Orca**

6. Go to [Orca.so](https://www.orca.so/).
7. Connect your wallet.
8. Navigate to **"Pools"**.
9. Create a liquidity pool with `GIVE` and SOL/USDC.
10. Confirm the transaction.

---

### **3. Register the Token with Jupiter**

Jupiter pulls liquidity directly from Raydium and Orca, so once you provide liquidity, Jupiter will automatically detect your token pair.

To verify:

11. Go to [Jupiter Aggregator](https://jup.ag/).
12. Use the **token address** to search for GIVE in the trade interface.
13. If the token doesn’t appear:
    - Wait a few minutes for the DEX to sync with Jupiter.
    - Manually add your token to the wallet by entering its address.

---

### **4. Promote and Use Your Token**

Once liquidity is live on Jupiter:

- Users can trade `GIVE` directly on Jupiter using the trading pair you created.
- Share the **Jupiter link** to promote your token.

---

### **Summary**

14. Add liquidity for **GIVE** on a Solana DEX (Raydium or Orca).
15. Jupiter will automatically detect your token pair.
16. Verify and trade using Jupiter’s interface.
