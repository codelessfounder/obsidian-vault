### **Minting 8.12 Billion Coins on Solana**

### **Step 1: Token Creation**

1. Use the Solana **SPL Token CLI** to create the token:
    - Command: `spl-token create-token`
    - This initializes the token with the ability to mint a specific total supply.
2. **Cost**:
    - The Solana network charges a minimal fee (typically 0.002 SOL, or ~$0.05) to create a token.

---

### **Step 2: Mint the Total Supply**

3. Define the total supply of **8.12 billion GIVE** tokens:
    - Command: `spl-token mint <TOKEN_ADDRESS> 8120000000`
    - This action mints all 8.12 billion tokens to your chosen wallet(s).
4. **Cost**:
    - Minting tokens involves another minimal Solana transaction fee (0.002 SOL, or ~$0.05).
