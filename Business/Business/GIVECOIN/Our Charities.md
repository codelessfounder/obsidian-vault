Givecoin (GIVE) is committed to supporting charities that align with the principles of Effective Altruism, focusing on transparency, cost-effectiveness, and evidence-based interventions. Based on recommendations from Effective Altruism Australia

[Effective Altruism](https://effectivealtruism.org.au/evaluating-charities/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

, GIVE will direct donations to the following organizations:

1. **Against Malaria Foundation (AMF)**
    - **Mission**: Distributes long-lasting insecticidal nets to prevent malaria, a leading cause of illness and death in many developing countries.
    - **Impact**: AMF's interventions have been shown to significantly reduce malaria incidence, particularly among vulnerable populations such as children under five.
2. **Malaria Consortium**
    - **Mission**: Focuses on the prevention, control, and treatment of malaria and other communicable diseases in Africa and Asia.
    - **Impact**: Their Seasonal Malaria Chemoprevention (SMC) program has been highly effective in reducing malaria cases during peak transmission seasons.
3. **Helen Keller International**
    - **Mission**: Combats the causes and consequences of blindness and malnutrition by establishing vitamin A supplementation programs.
    - **Impact**: Vitamin A supplementation is a proven, cost-effective intervention that reduces child mortality and improves overall health outcomes.
4. **Evidence Action's Deworm the World Initiative**
    - **Mission**: Provides school-based deworming treatments to children in regions where parasitic worm infections are common.
    - **Impact**: Regular deworming improves children's health, nutrition, and cognitive development, leading to better educational outcomes.
5. **GiveDirectly**
    - **Mission**: Delivers unconditional cash transfers to individuals living in extreme poverty, allowing them to allocate funds according to their most pressing needs.
    - **Impact**: Cash transfers have been shown to improve recipients' economic status, health, and well-being, with sustained benefits over time.

By donating to these organizations, Givecoin ensures that contributions are utilized in the most effective manner, maximizing positive outcomes for those in need.
