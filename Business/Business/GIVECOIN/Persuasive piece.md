Traditional international donation systems often charge high fees for currency conversions and intermediary services. Decentralised cryptocurrencies like Givecoin allow donors to bypass these fees, ensuring that a higher percentage of their contributions goes directly to the cause.

**2. Global Accessibility**

Cryptocurrencies make it possible for anyone in the world to contribute to EA-aligned causes without needing access to traditional banking systems. This opens up a wider donor base, particularly in underbanked regions.

Unlike traditional cross-border donations, which can take days or even weeks, cryptocurrency transactions are nearly instantaneous. This speed is critical for time-sensitive aid efforts, ensuring funds reach charities faster.

Blockchain’s immutable ledger ensures that all transactions are visible and traceable, reducing the risk of fraud and increasing trust. Donors can verify their contributions in real-time, creating a level of accountability unmatched by traditional systems.

**5. Enhanced Donor Privacy**

For donors who value privacy, cryptocurrencies allow them to contribute without sharing extensive personal information, unlike traditional donation platforms.
