**This Non-Disclosure Agreement (“Agreement”)** is entered into as of [Date], by and between:

- **Disco Technologies Pty Ltd** (or equivalent entity once incorporated), with principal place of business at [Address] (“Disco”), and
- **[Counterparty Name]**, with principal place of business at [Address] (“Recipient”).

Together, the parties are referred to as the “Parties.”

---

## 1. Purpose

Disco is developing a proprietary platform that combines a **progressive online casino, PWA, and browser extension** with a **crypto-based gamified checkout experience**. In connection with discussions about a potential investment, partnership, service relationship, or other business dealings (“Purpose”), Disco may disclose certain confidential and proprietary information to Recipient.

---

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

“Confidential Information” includes, without limitation:

- Technical details of Disco’s platform, products, and services (e.g., PWA architecture, browser extension, gamification mechanics, crypto integrations, APIs, Supabase database schemas).
- Business models, strategies, pricing structures, expected value mechanics, risk frameworks, and regulatory positioning.
- Product roadmaps, prototypes, source code, UX/UI designs, brand assets, algorithms, and research data.
- Fundraising materials, investor decks, pitch content, financial information, customer/user data, and partner lists.
- Any non-public information marked “confidential” or that should reasonably be understood to be confidential.

Confidential Information does not include information that:

1. Is or becomes publicly available without breach of this Agreement;
2. Is rightfully received by Recipient from a third party without duty of confidentiality;
3. Is independently developed by Recipient without use of Disco’s Confidential Information.

---

## 3. Obligations of Recipient

Recipient agrees to:

- Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose.
- Maintain Confidential Information in strict confidence, with at least the same degree of care it uses to protect its own confidential materials (but never less than reasonable care).
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without Disco’s prior written consent, except to Recipient’s employees, officers, affiliates, or advisors who need to know for the Purpose and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
- Not copy, reverse engineer, disassemble, or otherwise attempt to derive the source of Disco’s technology.

---

## 4. Term & Return of Materials

This Agreement will remain in effect for **two (2) years** from the Effective Date, except that obligations regarding trade secrets will survive indefinitely.

Upon request, Recipient will promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information, including copies, summaries, or derivatives.

---

## 5. No License

Nothing in this Agreement grants Recipient any rights, title, or interest in or to Disco’s intellectual property, trademarks, trade secrets, or technology.

---

## 6. Remedies

Recipient acknowledges that unauthorised use or disclosure of Confidential Information may cause irreparable harm to Disco. Accordingly, Disco is entitled to seek injunctive relief, in addition to other remedies available at law or equity.

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding the subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements, whether oral or written. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.
