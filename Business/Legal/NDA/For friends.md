# **Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement (Personal Involvement)**

**This Non-Disclosure Agreement (“Agreement”)** is entered into as of [Date], by and between:

- **Harry Charlesworth** (“Disclosing Party”), residing at [Address], and
- **[Recipient Name]**, with principal place of residence at [Address] (“Recipient”).

Together, the parties are referred to as the “Parties.”

---

## 1. Purpose

The Disclosing Party is engaged in the creation, development, and/or involvement with a project currently known as **Disco** (the “Project”). In connection with discussions, collaboration, or awareness of the Project, Recipient may learn or already knows of the Disclosing Party’s involvement.

The Disclosing Party wishes to ensure that their association with the Project is kept strictly confidential.

---

## 2. Confidential Information

For purposes of this Agreement, “Confidential Information” means, without limitation:

- The fact that the Disclosing Party is associated with, works on, advises, invests in, or has any relationship to the Project.
- Any details about the nature of the Disclosing Party’s role, responsibilities, or involvement.
- Any communications, conversations, or references that could reasonably identify the Disclosing Party’s connection to the Project to others.

---

## 3. Obligations of Recipient

Recipient agrees that they shall:

1. **Not disclose** to any person, company, organisation, or other party that the Disclosing Party is associated with the Project.
2. **Not mention or imply** the Disclosing Party’s involvement with the Project in conversations, messages, emails, social media, or other communications.
3. **Take reasonable measures** to prevent accidental disclosure of this information.
4. Use the knowledge of the Disclosing Party’s involvement **solely for internal understanding** and for no other purpose.

---

## 4. Term

The obligations of confidentiality shall remain in effect for a period of **five (5) years** from the Effective Date, or indefinitely until the Disclosing Party makes their association with the Project public, whichever comes first.

---

## 5. Remedies

Recipient acknowledges that any unauthorised disclosure of the Disclosing Party’s association with the Project would cause irreparable harm. The Disclosing Party is entitled to seek injunctive relief and any other remedies available at law or equity.

---

## 6. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of **New South Wales, Australia**, without regard to its conflict-of-law principles.

---

## 7. Entire Agreement

This Agreement represents the entire understanding of the Parties regarding the confidentiality of the Disclosing Party’s involvement with the Project, and supersedes all prior oral or written understandings.
