### **What happens if you launch without compliance?**

2. **Risks**

    - **Banking/partners shutoff:** On-ramps, influencers, or marketing platforms may reject or ban you for operating unlicensed.

    - Many gambling startups launch “grey market” first under light/no licensing, then migrate operations offshore (e.g., Curacao, Anjouan, Philippines).
