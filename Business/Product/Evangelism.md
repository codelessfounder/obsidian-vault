Vision:

1. To help watch dealers sell watches 
2. To make it safer for enthusiasts to buy watches

- **Inventory: ***How can we further simplify the inventory experience to make managing watch details and availability even faster and more intuitive for our team and customers?*
- **Sharing: ***How can we design the shared inventory link to minimise information asymmetry and enhance transparency, building greater trust and confidence in our sellers among customers?*
- **Analytics: ***How might we leverage real-time metrics to enable quicker, more data-informed decisions, particularly in responding to offers and setting strategic performance goals?*



Watchcrow is in the business of saving time for watch sellers in the sales process. 

Watchcrow is in the business of making watch sellers more money. 

Watchcrow is in the business of making watch details accessible and easy to understand for watch buyers.

Watchcrow is in the business of reducing information asymmetry in transactions of luxury goods 


For enthusiasts: Watchcrow is a digital portfolio for your collection
For sellers: Watchcrow is an inventory management tool 

Watchcrow will replace dealer websites 

an all in one sales and marketing tool for watch dealers
