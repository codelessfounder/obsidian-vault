1. Increase in watch sales (%)

3. Increase in sell price ($)

User flow:

4. Seller creates ‘watch(es)’ in Inventory (dashboard)
5. Seller presses ‘share’ on inventory table 
6. Seller copy and pastes link from popup ‘share’ into their bio 
7. Buyers clicks on one of the watches in the list
8. Buyer pays a deposit 
9. Transaction is created 
10. Buyer and seller meet in person 
11. Buyer collects the watch pays the difference
12. Buyer releases deposit to the seller instantly 
13. Seller withdraws money from Watchcrow (minus fee)
