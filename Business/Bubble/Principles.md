1. **Organise the data in a structured way **
    - Spend the time to organise your data to minimise the amount of time spent between design, logic and database. 
    - What data types (tables) do you need?
    - What fields do you need?
    - How should records be related?
    - Where can you leverage option sets?
    - The database is the heart of your application 
2. **Spend less time on front end designs **
    - Focus on general layout, logic and data structure
    - Menu system - navigating between pages 
    - Styles - maintaining consistencies across your app easily 
3. **Learn how to monitor your app’s performance early on**
    - Learn metrics charts in your bubble editor 
    - Understand where the heavier areas of your app are
        - Consolidate workflows, query database efficiently, alternate database structures
4. **Form great organisational habits **
    - label element that you design 
    - create workflows folders 
    - name scheme for data types & option sets 
    - check issues regularly 
    - delete unnecessary elements
    - uninstall plugins that aren’t in use 
5. **Learn from the experiences of others!**
