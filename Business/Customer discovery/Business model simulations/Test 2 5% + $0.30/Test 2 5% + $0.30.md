- Run 1: Total profit **$178.00**, profit/transaction **$1.78**, store credit **$520**.
- Run 2: Total profit **$157.50**, profit/transaction **$1.57**, store credit **$530**.

Outcome mix trends:

- Discounts: ~24-26%
- Store credit: ~33-39%
- Full price: ~35-43%

Qualitative interpretation:

- ~61% of customers experience variable outcomes (discount or credit), supporting gamified checkout positioning.
- 5% + $0.30 can be viable while keeping merchant outcomes stable.
- Store credit acts as both a retention mechanism and a perceived win lever.
