[[Necessary Conveniences of Life in 2025 Sydney]]

A guide to doing the bare minimum 

Core routines:

- Washing clothes
- Being responsible with money
- Keeping the house clean
- Personal hygiene
- Hosting guests
- Doing the dishes
- Sleeping


System (How):

Frequency (When): 

Tools(With what):

Science (Why): 

1. **Managing Food Supplies**
    - **System**: Plan weekly meals and shopping trips.
    - **Tools**: Shopping list apps (e.g., AnyList), meal planners.
    - **Frequency**: Weekly.

2. **Maintaining Your Bed**
    - **System**: Make your bed every morning and change sheets regularly.
    - **Tools**: Good quality bedding, laundry detergent.
    - **Frequency**: Make daily; change sheets weekly.
3. **Paying Bills on Time**
    - **System**: Automate payments or use a calendar for reminders.
    - **Tools**: Budgeting apps (e.g., Mint, YNAB), online banking.
    - **Frequency**: Monthly.
4. **Cooking Basic Meals**
    - **System**: Learn 5-10 simple, versatile recipes.
    - **Tools**: Basic kitchenware, recipe apps (e.g., Tasty, BBC Good Food).
    - **Frequency**: Daily for meals.
5. **Tidying Up Regularly**
    - **System**: Use the "Clean as You Go" method or a 15-minute nightly reset.
    - **Tools**: Storage bins, multi-surface cleaner.
    - **Frequency**: Daily.
6. **Basic Time Management**
    - **System**: Block time for tasks and set priorities.
    - **Tools**: Calendars, to-do list apps (e.g., Google Calendar, Todoist).
    - **Frequency**: Plan weekly; review daily.
7. **Staying Hydrated**
    - **System**: Carry a water bottle and track daily intake.
    - **Tools**: Hydration apps, reusable water bottles.
    - **Frequency**: Daily.
8. **Basic Fitness**
    - **System**: Commit to short, achievable workouts.
    - **Tools**: Yoga mat, fitness apps (e.g., Nike Training Club).
    - **Frequency**: 3-5 times a week.
9. **Digital Cleanliness**
    - **System**: Organise files and delete unnecessary emails or apps.
    - **Tools**: Cloud storage services, file management apps.
    - **Frequency**: Monthly.
