## Skill Dexterity Framework

Skill dexterity is the ability to execute with precision, speed, coordination, and adaptability across physical, cognitive, and social domains.

### Types

1. Physical dexterity: fine and gross motor control (e.g. instruments, sport, tools).
2. Cognitive dexterity: fast thinking, problem-solving, and strategic adaptation.
3. Social dexterity: communication, persuasion, and conflict navigation.

### Core characteristics

- Precision
- Speed
- Adaptability
- Coordination

### Improvement methods

- Physical: practice motor-skill tasks, hand-strengthening, repetitive drills.
- Cognitive: puzzles, strategy games, quick decision-making reps.
- Social: active listening, empathy, and rehearsing hard conversations.
