### ✅ Must-Do Within the First Week:

16. **Full logbook service (if no recent records):**
    - **Engine oil + filter**
    - **Fuel filter** (critical for diesel engines)
    - **Air filter + cabin filter**
    - **Coolant flush**
    - **Brake fluid flush**
    - **Transmission fluid + filter** (automatic)
17. **Timing chain inspection**
    - Common wear item — if it's rattly on cold starts, get it checked.
18. **Check all belts and pulleys**
    - Look for cracking, squealing, or fraying.
19. **Inspect tyres, brakes, suspension**
    - Pads/discs, shock absorbers, and tyre tread — replace as needed.
20. **Scan for error codes**
    - Ask your mechanic to run an OBD2 diagnostic scan for hidden faults.

---

## 🧰 **PRIORITY 2: Preventative Work for Diesel Longevity**

21. **Clean or replace EGR valve** (common for buildup over time)
22. **Inspect intake manifold for carbon buildup**
    - Especially important in high km diesels
23. **Use diesel fuel system cleaner** (e.g. Liqui Moly Diesel Purge or equivalent)
    - Cleans injectors and fuel system.
24. **Check turbo health**
    - Listen for excessive whistle or delayed spool — small leaks can be caught early.
