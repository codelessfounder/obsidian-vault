## Warm-up and Prep

### Bench setup checklist

1. Grip the bar with a stable, flat-back position.
2. Plant feet firmly to create leg drive and stability.
3. Set eye line directly under the bar before unracking.
4. Unrack with control, then press through full reps.

### Movement prep list

- Deadbug
- Glute Bridge
- Lat Pull Downs
- Squat with Plate OR RDL Kettlebell
- Cable Flys
- Shoulder Plyo
